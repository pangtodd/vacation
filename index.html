Who needs a vacation?

On top of dealing with the pandemic, Pacific NW residents have been subjected to wildfires, ice storms, and a ridiculous heat dome. I think we're all ready for a change of scenery. Where to?

Zanzibar

There are many island destinations, but does any sound cooler than Zanzibar? I've never been (and may never get there), but I still love saying it out loud. Zan-zi-bar!

"Zanzibar is a marvel for the senses. Every sight and smell is provocative, inspiring a sense of the old and new."
